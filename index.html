🌍 About Jobridge International

Jobridge International is a recruitment initiative based in Madagascar. We connect motivated workers with legal international job opportunities in agriculture, construction, transport, and services.

🔒 Transparency & Trust

We operate with full transparency. No hidden fees. No illegal recruitment practices. All candidates are verified before being connected to partners.
